Frozen Mai Tai

Description

Living in Hawaii at Ko O'lina for a decade, my frozen mai tai recipe is as authentic as it is tasty. 'Ono-licious!

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
  • 1/2 cup white rum
  • 1/2 cup dark rum
  • 1/2 cup amaretto liqueur
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur
    Steps
  1. Dissolve sugar in water in a saucepan over low heat, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Pour frozen limeade, white rum, dark rum, amaretto liqueur, and orange liqueur into sugar mixture. Mix well.
  3. Pour mixture into a bowl and freeze for at least 1 hour.
  4. Scoop frozen concoction into cocktail glasses and serve.

Cook's Note

You can also pour the mixture into a glass pitcher and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours instead of freezing. Serve over ice in cocktail glasses.
